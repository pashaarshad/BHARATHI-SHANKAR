About Me:


With a Bachelor's in Mass Communication and a Master's in Sales and Marketing,
I bring over 6 years of experience in sales and marketing, having worked across B2C, B2B,
and partnership roles. Passionate about training, I have been helping individuals develop
essential soft skills for over a decade. I understand firsthand how soft skills can make or break a career.

I firmly believe that mastering these skills is essential for both personal growth and professional success.

My mission is to help individuals reach their fullest potential by enhancing the core soft skills that empower them to succeed in all aspects of life and open doors to new opportunities.
